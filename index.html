Interactive Visualization of the Building Inventory Dataset

Below is an example of an interactive visualization using vega-lite. The first graph shows the total square footage acquired by each congressional district. The second chart is a scatter plot showing the relationship between the year constructed and the year acquired

The interativity is seen when a certain congressional district is selected in the chart one, chart 2 will highlight the specific years when the square footage that belong to that particular congressional district were contructed and acquired.

I think this is a very interesting interactive visualization.
